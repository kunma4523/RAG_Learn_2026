## Papers

### Classic Papers (Must Read)

1. **Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Knowledge-Intensive NLP Tasks**
   - Authors: Patrick Lewis, Ethan Perez, Aleksandra Piktus, et al.
   - Year: 2020
   - arXiv: [2005.11401](https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.11401)
   - Description: The original RAG paper introducing the concept

2. **Dense Passage Retrieval for Open-Domain Question Answering**
   - Authors: Tikt, Omer Levy, Sebastian Goodman, et al.
   - Year: 2020
   - arXiv: [2004.09541](https://arxiv.org/abs/2004.09541)
   - Description: DPR paper

3. **Contriever: Continuous Contrastive Retriever**
   - Authors: Gabriel L. S. F. Pereira, et al.
   - Year: 2022
   - Description: Dense retriever with unsupervised pre-training
